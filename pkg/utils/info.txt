Bot for learning english words with interval repetition method. Check https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaced_repetition for info.
You add a word and then get notification with it within such intervals: 20 minutes, 1 hour, 8 hours, 1 day and 3 days.

Available commands:
/start - creates new base of words if you are new user, if not - does nothing
/info - prints this text :)
/add - adds word to your base. The format of input should be: /add word - translation
/delete - deletes specific word with given translation. The format of input should be: /delete word - translation
/drop - deletes your whole base
